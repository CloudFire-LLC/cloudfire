# Deploy Firezone

This detailed, step-by-step guide will walk you through an end-to-end deployment
of Firezone for your organization. It's geared towards administrators and other
technical staff who are responsible for deploying and maintaining Firezone for
their team.

  Looking to get up and running with a basic deployment for testing? See our

</Alert>

## Get started

1. [Create a Site](/kb/deploy/sites): A site is a logical grouping of resources
   and gateways.
1. [Deploy Gateways](/kb/deploy/gateways): Gateways connect users to resources.
1. [Create Resources](/kb/deploy/resources): Resources are the things you wish
   to manage access to.
1. [Create Groups](/kb/deploy/groups): Groups organize users to determine which
   policies apply to them.
1. [Create Users](/kb/deploy/users): Users are admins and end-users of your
   Firezone deployment.
1. [Create Policies](/kb/deploy/policies): Policies define which groups can
   access which resources.
1. [Install Clients](/kb/deploy/clients): Clients are used by end-users to
   access resources.
1. [Configure DNS](/kb/deploy/dns)
